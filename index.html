How to Play

01

Setup

The host picks a category (Colors, Fruits, Animals…). Each item appears exactly 3 times plus one special ⚡ BOOST card.

02

Dealing

Every player gets 3 cards. One random player also receives the BOOST card, giving them 4 cards to start.

03

Passing

Players pass one card at a time in the chosen direction. You cannot immediately pass back the card you just received.

04

Winning

Collect 3 identical cards and immediately press the BOOST button. First valid player wins the round!

⚠️

Penalties

False BOOST = elimination. Invalid move = warning. 3 warnings = elimination.

⚡

BOOST Card

The BOOST card rotates after each round. Track who holds it — a badge shows the current owner.
